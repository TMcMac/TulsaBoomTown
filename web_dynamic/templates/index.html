Oppertunities to Learn and Grow

Tulsa Remote:

You're looking for something new, Tulsa Remote is looking for great people to join the Tulsa community. Currently offering $10,000 cash, free desk space, exclusive perks / events and of course, a welcoming community.

Holberton School:

Located in the Downtown Tulsa Arts District, Holberton School is Oklahoma's premiere coding school. Campus facilities are open to students 24hrs a day, 7 days a week. They offer a spacious computer lab with over 30 work stations and various conference rooms, quiet spaces and nooks. Learn by doing, learn from each other!

36D North:

36 Degrees North is the central gathering point for Tulsa's startup founders, small business owners, remote workers and innovative community leaders. We provide high-quality workspace in the Tulsa Arts District, foster a tight-knit community, and curate connections to helpful resources, so entrepreneurs can grow and create long-lasting, impactful businesses.

5th Floor:

New incubator, coming soon, brought to you by 36 and city of Tulsa. A plan has been proposed to use $1.25 million in CARES Act funding to create a flexible work space on the fifth floor of City Hall. 36 Degrees North, which has sites in the Tulsa Arts District, would pay about $480,000 a year in rent.

What Tulsa has to Offer

Historic Route 66

Traveling on Route 66 might be the most iconic road trip route in the U.S. Known as “The Mother Road”. Route 66 made inter-continental travel from Illinois to Los Angeles possible for millions. In Oklahoma, Route 66 spans from the north-eastern to south-central portions of the state. A few highlights include: The Oklahoma Route 66 Museum in Clinton, The Coleman Theatre in Miami, The Blue Whale in Catoosa, Totem Pole Park in Chelsea, The Seaba Station Motorcycle Museum, Homestead on Route 66 in Yukon.

The Gathering Place

We are Gathering Place. We remain consistent in our mission, as a project led by George Kaiser Family Foundation, that we all become united in our love for our community. Relatives, close friends, neighbors, we are all family. We are united from every zip code, from the city to the suburbs, from the river to the hills, from the north to the south, from the west to the east. Gathering Place is the ultimate expression of Tulsa’s spirit of unity. A place for everyone.

Live Music, Arts & Culture

Tulsa's rich and vibrant music & arts scene paves the way for our local communities culture to flourish. Tulsa's local music and art help to build our economy, improve our education system, help to preserve our local heritage and culture, and provide an over all enhanced quality of life for all to enjoy.
